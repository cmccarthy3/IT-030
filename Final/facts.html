Facts

  • Giraffes only need 5-30 minutes of sleep in a 24-hour period.
  • Elephants can recognize themselves in a mirror - a sign of self-awareness.
  • Octopuses have three hearts and blue blood.
  • Dolphins have name for one another and can call each other by a unique whistle.
  • Sea otters often hold hands when they sleep.
  • Cheetahs are the fast land animals, running up to 80mph.
