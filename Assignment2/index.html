Delicious Homemade Pancakes

Welcome to my simple breakfast recipe website, where you will find a grocery list and a recipe for making homemade pancakes.

Grocery List

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Maple Syrup

Recipe Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk eggs and milk together.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a pan and melt some butter.
  5. Pour batter into the pan and cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  6. Cook until golden brown and serve with maple syrup.
